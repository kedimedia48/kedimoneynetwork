KEDI — Business Profile

Kimina k’Ikoranabuhanga / Kedi Money Network — Kwizigama, Ubuhinzi, Ubworozi, n’Ubucuruzi binyuze mu ikoranabuhanga
Company Overview

KEDI (Kimina k’Ikoranabuhanga / Kedi Money Network) ni urubuga rw’ikoranabuhanga rugamije guhuza abantu mu muco wo kwizigama, gufashanya no kugabana inyungu. KEDI itanga serivisi za digital savings, imishinga yateza imbere ubuhinzi n’ubworozi, ndetse n’ubucuruzi bwifashisha ikoranabuhanga (e-commerce & agri-commerce). Platformi yacu ifasha abanyamuryango kubitsa, gusaba inguzanyo, kubona amahugurwa n'ubufasha mu bucuruzi no mu buhinzi.

Vision & Mission
Vision
Kuba urubuga nyafurika nyambere mu bucuruzi bwo kwizigama, ubuhinzi n'ubworozi byubakiye ku ikoranabuhanga — bituma abaturage bagira imibereho myiza n’iterambere rirambye.
Mission
  • Guhuza abantu binyuze mu ikoranabuhanga kugira ngo bigire akamaro mu mikorere yo kwizigamira no gufashanya.
  • Kuzamura imishinga y’ubuhinzi n’ubworozi binyuze mu mishinga y’imari (microloans), amahugurwa na agri-tech solutions.
  • Kuzamura ubucuruzi bwifashisha ikoranabuhanga (agri-commerce) kugira ngo ibicuruzwa by’abaturage bigere ku isoko.
  • Gutanga platform ifite umutekano, yoroshye gukoresha kandi igezweho mu bikorwa by’imari n’ubucuruzi.
Core Values
  • Ubufatanye: Gukorera hamwe nk’umuryango.
  • Ubunyangamugayo: Gukorera mu mucyo mu micungire y’amafaranga n’amakuru.
  • Ikoranabuhanga: Gukoresha ibisubizo bishya byoroshya ubuzima n’imikorere.
  • Kwizigama & Iterambere: Guteza imbere umuco wo kwizigama no gushyigikira imishinga yinjiza inyungu.
  • Guterimbere rusange: Gushyira imbere inyungu z’umuryango wose.
Products & Services
KEDI Money Network
Serivisi zo kwizigama, kubitsa, n’imicungire y’inyungu hifashishijwe tree network system.
Agri & Livestock Support
KEDI ishyigikira imishinga y'ubuhinzi n'ubworozi binyuze mu buryo bukurikira:
  • Microloans (inguzanyo zoroheje) ku bahinzi n’abororoka bagamije kongera umusaruro.
  • Amahugurwa mu by’ubuhinzi bugezweho (agri-tech), imicungire y’amatungo, no ku buryo bwo guhinga butangiza ibidukikije.
  • Agri-commerce: gufasha abahinzi kugera ku masoko abaguzi hifashishijwe platformi, logistics na payment integration (MoMo/Bank).
  • Insurance partnerships (aho bishoboka) no gutanga inama ku micungire y’ibihingwa n’amatungo kugira ngo hatangizwa ingaruka z’imihindagurikire y’ikirere.
KEDI Academy
Amahugurwa na bootcamps mu ikoranabuhanga (coding, fintech, agri-tech) kugira ngo urubyiruko n’abikorera bato babone ubumenyi bukenewe.
KEDI Support & Community Programs
Ubufasha ku bakiriya, gahunda z’iterambere ry’imiryango, ndetse n’ibikorwa byo gukangurira abaturage kwizigamira no kwihangira imirimo.
Target Market
  • Abahinzi n’abororoka bifuza kubona inguzanyo, amasoko no kongera umusaruro.
  • Abaturage bashaka uburyo bworoshye bwo kwizigama no kubona inguzanyo mu mucyo.
  • Amakoperative, amatsinda n’ibimina bifite intego yo gucunga umutungo mu buryo bugezweho.
  • Urubyiruko n’abiga ikoranabuhanga bifuza amahugurwa n’amahirwe y’umwuga.
Competitive Advantage
  • Platform yizewe, yoroshye gukoresha, kandi ihuza amafaranga, ubucuruzi n’ubuhinzi.
  • Tree network system itanga ubusobanuro bw’aho buri wese ahagaze n’inyungu ze.
  • Integration na mobile money na banki ku rwego rw’igihugu (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Amabanki) kugira ngo amasezerano yo kwishyura n’amasoko byorohere.
  • Porogaramu zo gushyigikira ubuhinzi n’ubworozi (microloans, amahugurwa, agri-commerce) zihuza imari n’isoko.
Future Outlook (5 years)
  • Abanyamuryango ibihumbi mu Rwanda no mu karere, cyane mu midugudu yibanda ku buhinzi n’ubworozi.
  • Korana na amabanki, ibigo by’ubwishingizi n’abashoramari mu rwego rwo gukura ibikorwa by’imari.
  • Gutanga application mobile igera kuri buri wese ku bikorwa bya buri munsi by’imari n’ubucuruzi.
  • Kuzamura agri-tech solutions zifasha abahinzi kongera umusaruro no kugabanya igihombo.
